Ormoc's Pineapple

The pineapple known as Ormoc's queen is small in size, prickly, and rough on the outside, yet juicy and delicious within. The pina is significant because Ormoc is renowned for its excellent pineapples. This city has been known for producing pineapples in the Visayas for the longest period.


Can be found in: Ormoc's Pier, Ormoc Supermarketsm and fruit stands near Shell Gasoline Station
Price Range: Php 25.00 per fruit
Binagol

Binagol is a delicacy prepared from the root crop known as "Taro" or "Gabi" in our language, which is Filipino. The term "bagol" (which signifies coconut shell) is where the name Binagol originated. It comes in a special package and has a particular flavor for the palate.


Can be found in: Ormoc's Pier
Price Range: Php 35.00 per piece.(Can bargain for Php 100.00 for 3 pieces.)
Cassava Cake

Grated cassava (manioc) is the main ingredient in Cassava Cake, a traditional Filipino treat. In the Philippines, cassava is also referred to as kamoteng kahoy and balanghoy.


Can be found in: Ormoc's Best Cassava Shop and Yoyi's Cakes & Pastries
Price Range: Php 250.00 per box
Chocolate Moron

Moron is a rice cake that resembles suman in our cuisine. Chocolate Moron, known as "malagkit" (ground glutinous rice), is a chocolate treat that is cooked in coconut milk. You may eat moron straight or combined with chocolate or cocoa.

Can be found in: Ormoc's Pier
Price Range: Php 100.00 for 5-4 plastic pieces. (5 chocolate moron per plastic)
